How BootPay Works

Our seamless ecosystem connects boot fair organizers, stallholders, and customers in one comprehensive platform.

1

Organizer Setup

Boot fair organizers create events, set stall prices, and configure entrance fees through their dedicated dashboard.

2

Stallholder Registration

Stallholders book and pay for their spaces in advance or on the day, receiving digital receipts and location assignments.

3

Customer Arrival

Visitors pay entrance fees through the app or at the gate, receiving digital tickets for fast-track entry.

4

Seamless Transactions

Customers make cashless purchases using NFC tap-to-pay or through their BootPay accounts.
